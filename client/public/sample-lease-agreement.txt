RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on January 1, 2026, between:

LANDLORD: Sharma Properties LLP, having its registered office at 42 Nehru Marg, Bangalore 560001 (hereinafter referred to as the "Landlord").

TENANT: ______________________, residing at ________________________ (hereinafter referred to as the "Tenant").


1. PREMISES
The Landlord agrees to lease the residential property located at Flat 3B, Lotus Apartments, MG Road, Bangalore 560001 (the "Premises") to the Tenant under the terms stated herein.


2. TERM
The lease shall commence on January 1, 2026, and continue for a period of 11 months, ending on November 30, 2026. The lease shall not automatically renew under any circumstances.


3. RENT
The monthly rent shall be Rupees 35,000 (Thirty-Five Thousand), due on the 1st day of each calendar month. Late payment shall incur a penalty of Rupees 500 per day for the first 7 days, and Rupees 1,000 per day thereafter until full payment is received. Repeated late payment will result in immediate eviction without refund.


4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall deposit a sum of Rupees 3,50,000 (equivalent to 10 months' rent) as security. This deposit is non-refundable if the Tenant vacates before the end of the lease term for any reason whatsoever, including medical emergencies, job relocation, or family crisis.


5. ENTRY BY LANDLORD
The Landlord and their authorized agents may enter the Premises at any time of the day or night, with or without prior notice to the Tenant, for inspection, maintenance, showing the Premises to prospective tenants, or any other purpose deemed necessary by the Landlord at their sole discretion.


6. TENANT LIABILITY
The Tenant shall be solely and fully liable for all damages to the Premises, including but not limited to normal wear and tear, acts of God, accidental damage, fire, flood, earthquake, and damage caused by any third party visitors or trespassers. The Tenant shall also be liable for any structural issues discovered during their tenancy, regardless of cause, origin, or when such issues first arose.


7. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Tenant is solely responsible for all repairs and maintenance costs, including but not limited to major repairs to plumbing, electrical systems, wiring, roofing, walls, and structural elements. The Landlord bears no obligation to maintain the Premises in any livable or habitable condition, and shall not be liable for any consequences arising from disrepair.


8. SUBLETTING AND GUESTS
The Tenant shall not sublet the Premises or host any overnight guests without prior written approval from the Landlord. Unauthorized guests, including family members staying more than one night, will result in immediate termination of this Agreement and forfeiture of the security deposit.


9. TERMINATION BY LANDLORD
The Landlord reserves the absolute right to terminate this Agreement at any time with just 7 days' written notice, for any reason or no reason at all, without any liability or compensation to the Tenant.


10. TERMINATION BY TENANT
The Tenant must provide 60 days' prior written notice to terminate this Agreement, and must additionally pay 3 months' rent as an early termination penalty, regardless of the circumstances, including medical emergencies or landlord-caused issues.


11. UTILITIES AND CHARGES
The Tenant shall pay for all utilities including water, electricity, gas, and internet. The Tenant shall also pay maintenance charges of Rupees 8,000 per month regardless of occupancy, use of common facilities, or whether such facilities are functional.


12. RENT ESCALATION
The Landlord reserves the right to increase the rent by any amount, at any time, with 15 days' notice. Refusal to pay the increased rent will result in immediate eviction.


13. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Karnataka, India. All disputes shall be resolved exclusively through binding arbitration at a venue chosen solely by the Landlord, with all arbitration costs and legal fees borne entirely by the Tenant, regardless of the outcome of the dispute.


14. INDEMNIFICATION
The Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Landlord from any and all claims, damages, injuries, deaths, or legal costs arising from the Tenant's use or occupancy of the Premises, including claims by third parties and any injuries sustained on the Premises by any person for any reason.


15. MODIFICATION OF TERMS
The Landlord reserves the right to modify any terms of this Agreement at any time, with changes taking effect immediately upon written or verbal notice to the Tenant. Continued occupancy of the Premises shall constitute acceptance of the modified terms.


16. WAIVER OF RIGHTS
The Tenant hereby waives all rights to legal recourse, class action participation, and statutory tenant protections as may be available under applicable rent control or tenancy laws.


17. ACCEPTANCE
By signing below, the Tenant acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all terms of this Agreement in its entirety, and further confirms that they have not been coerced or pressured into signing.
