RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT — REVISED VERSION

This Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on March 15, 2026, between:

LANDLORD: Sharma Properties LLP, having its registered office at 42 Nehru Marg, Bangalore 560001 (hereinafter referred to as the "Landlord").

TENANT: ______________________, residing at ________________________ (hereinafter referred to as the "Tenant").


1. PREMISES
The Landlord agrees to lease the residential property located at Flat 3B, Lotus Apartments, MG Road, Bangalore 560001 (the "Premises") to the Tenant under the terms stated herein.


2. TERM
The lease shall commence on April 1, 2026, and continue for a period of 11 months, ending on February 28, 2027. The lease may be renewed upon mutual written agreement.


3. RENT
The monthly rent shall be Rupees 42,000 (Forty-Two Thousand), due on the 5th day of each calendar month. A grace period of 5 days is allowed. Late payment beyond grace period shall incur a penalty of Rupees 300 per day, capped at Rupees 5,000 per month.


4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall deposit a sum of Rupees 1,26,000 (equivalent to 3 months' rent) as security. This deposit is refundable within 30 days of vacating the Premises, after deducting legitimate damages only (excluding normal wear and tear).


5. ENTRY BY LANDLORD
The Landlord and their authorized agents may enter the Premises for inspection or maintenance only after providing the Tenant with at least 24 hours prior written notice, except in cases of genuine emergency.


6. TENANT LIABILITY
The Tenant shall be liable for damages caused by their negligence or intentional misuse of the Premises. The Tenant shall NOT be liable for normal wear and tear, acts of God (earthquakes, floods, fire due to external causes), or damages arising from structural issues existing prior to the tenancy.


7. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Landlord shall be responsible for major repairs including plumbing, electrical systems, and structural elements. The Tenant is responsible only for minor day-to-day upkeep such as replacing light bulbs and keeping the premises clean.


8. SUBLETTING AND GUESTS
The Tenant may host overnight guests for up to 14 consecutive days without prior notice. Subletting requires prior written approval from the Landlord, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.


9. TERMINATION BY LANDLORD
The Landlord may terminate this Agreement only for material breach by the Tenant, upon providing 30 days' written notice and an opportunity to cure the breach.


10. TERMINATION BY TENANT
The Tenant may terminate this Agreement by providing 30 days' prior written notice, with no early termination penalty after the first 6 months of the lease.


11. UTILITIES AND CHARGES
The Tenant shall pay for electricity, gas, and internet. Water and common area maintenance charges shall be borne by the Landlord.


12. RENT ESCALATION
The rent shall remain fixed for the entire 11-month lease term. Any increase upon renewal shall not exceed 10% of the existing rent.


13. MANDATORY ARBITRATION AND CLASS ACTION WAIVER
All disputes shall be resolved exclusively through binding individual arbitration under the rules of the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act. The Tenant expressly waives the right to participate in any class action, collective action, or representative proceeding. Arbitration shall occur in Bangalore; each party bears their own legal fees.


14. INDEMNIFICATION
The Tenant agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Landlord from claims arising directly from the Tenant's negligent or intentional acts. The Landlord retains liability for injuries caused by the Premises' condition or Landlord's negligence.


15. PROPERTY INSPECTION FEE
A non-refundable property inspection fee of Rupees 5,000 shall be paid by the Tenant at the time of signing, and annually thereafter for continued occupancy.


16. AUTOMATIC BACKGROUND CHECKS
The Landlord reserves the right to perform periodic background checks, credit checks, and employment verification on the Tenant at any time during the tenancy. The Tenant hereby consents to such checks and waives any right to prior notification.


17. MODIFICATION OF TERMS
Any modification of this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties. No oral modifications shall be valid.


18. ACCEPTANCE
By signing below, both parties acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all terms of this Agreement in its entirety.
